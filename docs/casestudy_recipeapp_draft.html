About

Cooking is something all of us have to do and optimizing the process of finding recipes is essential. The main idea of the app is to help busy adults find simple and healthy recipes based only on what they have in their kitchen at the moment. It also allows users to constantly update their ingredients making it even easier just to see what recipes they can make at any given moment.

Challenge

People have trouble finding easy and available recipes without spending a lot of time browsing through various websites and apps, only to realize that they lack some important ingredients.

Goals

1. Design an intuitive mobile app where users add ingredients they have at home and see options on what they can cook with these particular ingredients. 2. Help busy people spend as little time as possible on choosing what to cook and how to cook daily meals.
